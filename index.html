About

Malala YousafZai

Pakistani education activist

Malala Yousafzai is a Pakistani female education activist and the 2014 Nobel Peace Prize laureate at the age of 17. She is the world's youngest Nobel Prize laureate, the second Pakistani and the first Pashtun to receive a Nobel Prize.

Born: 12 July 1997 (age 26 years), Mingora, Pakistan

From March 2013 to July 2017, Yousafzai was a pupil at the all-girls Edgbaston High School in Birmingham.In August 2015, she received 6 A*s and 4 As at GCSE level.At A-Level, she studied Geography, History, Mathematics and Religious Studies
